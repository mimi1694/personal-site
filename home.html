About

Mimi Scott is JavaScript developer currently living in New York City. She received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science from Hobart and William Smith Colleges, and further pursued web development by attending the Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy. She loves to spend her free time hiking in Maine, running half marathons with New York Road Runners, and has recently taken up woodworking. Mimi loves to stay busy and is always looking out for the next exciting project.

For further information on Fullstack Academy and the Grace Hopper program, check out their site.
